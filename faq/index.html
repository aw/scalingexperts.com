Who wrote this eBook?

The author is Alex Williams, although it is published under “The Scaling Experts” due to the collaboration of many expert system/network administrators in the IT industry.

Who is it for?

The “Web Scaling vol. 1” eBook is for people just getting started in the world of scalability. It is mainly written for supporters of Open Source Software wanting to grow their infrastructure without incurring incredibly high costs.

How much do you love Open Source?

We love Open Source Software so much, that we promise to donate part of the eBook sales proceeds to the Free Software Foundation.

As of December 31, 2012 we are glad to confirm our donation to the FSF.

Why isn’t the eBook free or Open Source?

How scalable is this website?

Wouldn’t it be ironic if a website for scaling was unavailable due to scaling issues? Well, no. For me it would be quite humiliating, but the truth is it’s extremely difficult to justify the purchase of a scalable infrastructure for this eBook’s website, until it becomes more popular and starts generating more revenue. For the moment, I’ll do my best to make sure it’s “always up“, but the goal of this website isn’t necessarily to serve thousands of viewers per day. In fact, if this website isn’t available for a long period of time, it’s likely (and unfortunately) caused by malicious attackers.
